Schedule Your Collection

Waste Collection

Efficiently managing your waste collection schedule ensures timely disposal, which is crucial for maintaining a clean and healthy living environment. Mark your collection days on a calendar or set reminders to avoid missing pickups. Separate waste properly by using labeled bins for recyclables, organic waste, and general waste. Prepare waste by securely bagging it to prevent spillage and minimize odors, and flatten or crush items to save space.
